SIMPLE HOMEMADE BREAD RECIPE

This recipe provides a straightforward method for preparing homemade bread, using basic ingredients and clear steps to help new bakers develop confidence and skill.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Serving: 1 loaf
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredient

  • 6 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoon yeast
  • 4 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instruction

  1. Combine flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Mix yeast with warm water and allow it to activate.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and oil to the dry ingredients.
  4. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic
  5. bake in a preheated oven at 180Â°C for 30-35 minutes.

Tips / Notes

Tips: for a golden curst, brush then top with melted butter right after baking.
